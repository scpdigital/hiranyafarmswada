About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Hiranya Farm. Our lives have taken us through the world of cinema, where stories like Harishchandrachi Factory, Elizabeth Ekadashi and Vaalvi brought us national and international recognition. Yet, even as awards and applause came our way, our hearts were always drawn back to something quieter, simpler, and perhaps even more meaningful — our love for nature and animals. I (Madhugandha) have often rescued animals from difficult situations, carrying a dream of one day creating a space where they could live freely and safely. Paresh, too, has always been deeply connected with greenery and conservation, guiding young people to value the world we share.

That dream slowly grew into reality here at Hiranya Farm in Wada, Maharashtra. Today, this is home not just for us, but also for our three loving dogs — Shiva, Shambhu, and Devi — and our two gentle adopted cows, Lakshmi and Saraswati, who have become part of our family. This farm is a place where you can pause, breathe deeply, spend time with animals, and let nature wrap you in its calm. When you visit, we don’t just hope you have a holiday — we hope you feel the warmth of being welcomed into a story, one that celebrates kindness, simplicity, and the joy of living close to the earth.
